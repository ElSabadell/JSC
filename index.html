THE GREAT
EUROPEAN
TEXTILE TRADITION

Jordi Sabadell has over 30 years of work in the textile world, collaborating directly with some of the most important manufacturers and producers of textile machinery in Europe and the rest of the world. This thirty-year experience has taught me the importance of continuous adaptation to new standards of textile manufacturers, thus bringing innovative, practical and productive solutions. My philosophy is to get the most out of my customers machines, making them more efficient and productive at the same time.

Assembly

We offer technical assistance for both new and used machines. We also follow the growth of the machine from assembly to packaging and finally shipping.

Changes

We also offer the possibility of making changes to textile machines of a software and hardware nature. We also carry out updates to all types of machines.

Repair

We offer 360 ° maintenance on textile machines, with scheduled but also extraordinary updates. In addition, we also carry out a restoration to new.

World Wide Services

Our thirty-year service is available in every part of the world.
